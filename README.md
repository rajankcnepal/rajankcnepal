## 👤 About Me

I am a **geotechnical engineer and researcher** with a focus on slope stability, earthquake geotechnics, deep foundation systems, disaster risk reduction, and infrastructure resilience in the Hindu Kush Himalayan region. I hold an **MSc in Geotechnical Engineering (Distinction, Batch Topper)** from the Institute of Engineering, Tribhuvan University (2022), and a **BE in Civil Engineering** (2019) from the same institution.

I am the **Managing Director of Geotechnical Associates Pvt. Ltd.** and a core team member at **Geoinfra Research Institute Pvt. Ltd.** (Chakupat-11, Lalitpur), where I lead geotechnical investigations, slope stabilization projects, and applied research. I also serve as a **Visiting Lecturer** at Universal Engineering and Science College (affiliated with Pokhara University), supervising MSc dissertations and conducting postgraduate courses.

**Recognition:**
